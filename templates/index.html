Why ML?

Machine Learning is used because bike rental demand is not linear. It depends on multiple factors like time, day, weather, season, and user behavior. ML models can learn hidden patterns from historical data and adapt to changes better than traditional rule-based systems.

Usage

The system uses historical ride and weather data to predict future demand. These predictions help operators: Prepare bikes in advance Avoid shortages during peak hours Reduce idle bikes during low demand.

Aim

The main goal is to make bike rental operations efficient and cost-effective. By knowing demand early, businesses can: Optimize bike distribution Improve customer satisfaction Reduce operational losses.
